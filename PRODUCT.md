# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Ganansya primarily serves sari-sari store operators, especially tindera users on mobile
phones who may be 40+ years old and have limited technical familiarity. They need to
perform cash-in and cash-out work quickly, confidently, and with minimal confusion during
real store operations.

Owner and admin users are secondary for the MVP. Their needs matter, especially monitoring,
pricing, reports, reconciliation, and audit visibility, but the first design priority is
making daily operator workflows simple and reliable.

## Product Purpose

Ganansya is a mobile-first cash-in, cash-out, and float-management system for sari-sari
stores. It helps operators log transactions, calculate fees, monitor cash and GCash
balances, avoid float problems, and reconcile daily activity.

Success means the tindera can complete common work without training-heavy steps: log a
transaction, confirm fees, see whether cash or GCash is low, and understand what to do next.

## Brand Personality

Trustworthy, practical, and complete.

Ganansya should feel dependable and grounded, like a tool made for real tindahan operations
rather than a polished fintech showcase. It should give users confidence that money, fees,
and balances are being handled correctly.

## Anti-references

Ganansya should not feel like a cluttered app with too many elements competing on one page.
It should avoid complex banking-app patterns, flashy fintech styling, dense dashboards,
crypto-wallet energy, and overloaded POS screens.

Minimalism is important, but not at the cost of operational clarity. The interface should
show enough for the tindera to act effectively, then keep secondary details out of the way
until needed.

## Design Principles

1. Tindera first: optimize every core screen for mobile store operators before admin complexity.
2. One clear action at a time: avoid crowding pages with competing tasks or dense controls.
3. Trust through clarity: make amounts, fees, balances, confirmations, and warnings unmistakable.
4. Complete without clutter: include the full workflow, but reveal details only when they help the current task.
5. Built for real phones: prioritize large tap targets, readable type, quick loading, and resilient layouts for budget Android devices.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use large tap targets, high contrast, simple Taglish labels, low motion, and WCAG AA where
practical. Optimize for 40+ non-technical users who may use the app in a bright store
environment, on small screens, and during busy customer interactions.
